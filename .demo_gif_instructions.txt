Demo GIF Instructions for Judges

Steps to Record:
1. Start demo (make demo or ./demo_run.sh)
2. Open demo UI at http://localhost:5173
3. Navigate to Upload page (/app/upload)
4. Upload examples/sample_invoice_1.png (or use any sample invoice)
5. Wait for OCR processing to complete
6. Click on a bounding box overlay region, edit a value (e.g., grand total)
7. Click "Accept & Send to Training" button
8. Scroll to Export panel (right sidebar)
9. Click "Download QuickBooks CSV"
10. Open the downloaded CSV in Excel/Google Sheets to show the data

Recording Tips:
- Use screen recording tool (OBS, QuickTime, or screen-to-gif)
- Keep recording to 30-45 seconds
- Highlight the ROI calculator showing savings
- Show the export download and CSV opening
- Attach the GIF to your PR or presentation
